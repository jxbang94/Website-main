What is Gaming Addiction

According to Cleveland Clinic, "Video game addiction, also called internet gaming disorder, is a condition characterized by severely reduced control over gaming habits, resulting in negative consequences in many aspects of your life, including self-care, relationships, school and work."





Why Gaming Addiction Happen

People are frequently drawn into gaming by the gratification of progress and the allure of in-game purchases. In addiction, the surge in dopamine levels following a gaming session encourages prolonged engagement, often leading individuals to play for extended periods.
